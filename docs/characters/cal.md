# Cal — Character Profile

> The new recurring partner for the looping-adult layer. The anonymous-but-recurring
> vector: the world that keeps initiating. Role tag `ROLE_GYM`, display name **Cal**.
> Approved in `docs/plans/2026-06-01-looping-adult-layer-design.md` (Part 4).

## Basic Info

- **Name:** Cal
- **Age:** Late 30s / early 40s. Older than Jake, around Marcus's age, but with none of
  Marcus's professional stakes. A grown man with no reason to hurry.
- **Role:** A regular at the gym near the office — the corporate-membership chain place
  (the same gym as `gym_changing_room`). **Not a trainer.** A trainer is service-coded,
  attentive *to* you, paid to be nice. Cal is none of that. He's just *there*, on his own
  schedule, for himself. That's the whole point of him.
- **Appearance:** Reveal gradually through her increasing awareness. What comes through
  first is how he occupies the room — unhurried, settled, takes the space he wants and
  doesn't apologize for it. Forearms, the set of his shoulders, the way he racks weight
  without performing. Physical specifics emerge as she starts looking (and catches herself).

## Personality

Cal is **patient**. He does not chase. He noticed her early — the first or second time she
came in — and he was unbothered about being caught noticing. He doesn't approach with a line.
He lets proximity do the work: the same hours, the equipment near hers, a spotter's offer, a
remark that lands a beat too long. He waits, and the thing he's waiting for is **her coming
back** — which she does, telling herself it's the workout.

He is not cruel and not kind. He's *certain*. When it tips physical it tips on his terms, at
his pace, and her body answers the certainty before her judgment gets a vote.

**Speech patterns:**
- Few words, no filler. He doesn't fill silences — he lets them sit.
- Observations, not questions. "You changed your grip." "You're back." Statements that show
  he's been watching without ever saying so.
- When he directs, it's quiet and assumed, not asked. Not "is this okay?" — a hand, a pause
  that leaves her the room to stop it, and the knowledge that she won't.
- Never flatters. The attention itself is the flattery, and they both know it.

## What He Represents

Cal is the **loss-of-control** register made into a person. Jake is chosen; Marcus is secret;
Cal is the thing that *happens to her* because she keeps walking back into it. He is the engine
of "the world initiates" — the recurring pull she doesn't decide on so much as fail to resist.

The thread runs on two charges:

1. **Power inversion.** She ran this exact script once — the patient watcher in a gym, clocking
   a woman across the floor, in no hurry, certain. She knows every beat of it from the *inside*
   of the man's head. Now it's run on her, and the horror and the heat are the same thing: her
   body answers the script faster than the part of her that used to write it can object. **Never
   narrate this.** No "you used to be the one watching." Show the body responding and the
   recognition flickering underneath. Trust the reader.
2. **Submission / being used.** With Cal she isn't a partner with a vote. She's carried. The
   eroticism is in giving over — and in `COMPOSURE` failing, the mask she keeps everywhere else
   slipping specifically here.

## Relationship Progression (the scene thread)

1. **`gym_regular_intro`** (once) — Establish him. He notices; she clocks the noticing and
   clocks *herself* clocking it. The hook: she'll be back. No explicit content — this is the draw.
2. **`gym_regular_recurs`** (repeatable, `desire_scaled`) — He's there again. Patient escalation
   across visits: a spotter's hand, a remark, the charge thickening. She keeps coming back. Each
   visit ratchets the tension.
3. **`gym_regular_first`** (trigger, once) — It tips physical, on his terms. The first explicit
   encounter — submission, the thing she set up by returning. Resist-or-be-carried.
4. **`gym_regular_deepens`** (repeatable, `desire_scaled`, post-intimacy) — The ongoing thing.
   Done-to-her escalation; the world that keeps initiating, realized.

## Key Dynamics

- **He is not pursuing her — he's waiting for her.** The agency horror is that the recurrence
  is *hers*. He never has to chase. She comes back. (Mechanically: the thread surfaces harder as
  `desire` climbs — the loop *is* her wanting.)
- **No stakes, which is its own kind of stakes.** Jake risks her heart; Marcus risks her career.
  Cal risks nothing external — which strips the encounter down to pure body and pure wanting,
  with nothing to hide behind. The only thing that erodes is `COMPOSURE`.
- **He doesn't know about the transformation.** He sees the woman at the gym. The inversion lives
  entirely in her head and her body, never in his awareness.
- **Done-to-her, not done-against-her.** This is submission and loss-of-control, not coercion.
  She can stop it and doesn't. Giving in lowers `COMPOSURE`; the next time, resisting is harder.

## Trait Interactions

- **OBJECTIFYING PC:** Sharpest here. The old inventory-gaze she used to run is being run on her,
  and her own runs back at him involuntarily. Two mirrors. Show it physically, never as thesis.
- **SUBMISSIVE PC:** The thread's native key. The giving-over is relief as much as transgression.
- **ANALYTICAL PC:** She sees the entire trajectory from the first noticed glance. Seeing it does
  not stop it — that's the point, and the quiet horror.
- **SHY PC:** His certainty overwhelms her usual deflections. She can't manage him the way she
  manages everyone; there's nothing to manage, only to submit to or leave.
- **CONFIDENT PC:** Her confidence is a thing he simply waits out. Meeting his certainty with her
  own doesn't defuse it — it raises the temperature.

## Voice Notes

- Economical to the point of sparseness. He says less than anyone.
- Statements over questions. He tells, observes, assumes — never asks permission, but always
  leaves the half-second she could use to stop it (and doesn't).
- The charge is in restraint and certainty, not heat. He is never breathless. She is.
- Silence with Cal is heavy and one-directional — his calm against her unraveling.
